The goal of the game is to maximize the time you stay alive.

Moving the sprite across a brown (mud) path costs you 4 seconds of time remaining.

Moving the sprite across a blue (ice) path costs you 5 seconds of time remaining.

Moving the sprite across a grey (regular road) path costs you 2 seconds of time remaining.

Moving the sprite across a white square adds the number(amount of time) on the square to your time remaining. The positions of the white squares are randomized.

Use the arrow keys to move the sprite.

Press a to begin.
